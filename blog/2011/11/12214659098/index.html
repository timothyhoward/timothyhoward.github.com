RIM made a video just like Mircosoft’s featuring such exciting innovations as requiring IT department approval before installing an app.

Some other incredible potential new features include remotely setting the security level of an employee’s phone to “level 5” and a phone which shows you a photo of yourself when you go through a security door. These are the kind of things RIM imagines when they’re fantasising about a future without factoring in a single real world constraint.

Best case scenario future concept videos are dumb but one that imagines something this boring sounds like RIM’s death knell.
