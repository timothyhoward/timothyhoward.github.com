Fairfax creating audiences in post-classified era

Fairfax CEO Greg Hywood doing nothing but repeating buzz words that neither he, nor anyone else understands. It’s mind boggling the extent to which Hywood doesn’t get it. He calls Fairfax’s iPad app “our iPad”, “there’s been 100,000 downloads of our iPad”. And I like this quote:

If you look at the old business, the old business didn’t really need the journalism.

Now there’s an imperative to produce quality journalism these media companies are really struggling.
