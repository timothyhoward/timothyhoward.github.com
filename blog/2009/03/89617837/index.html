EventBox is the non-HUD Twitter app I’ve been looking for. It has an incredible amount of configuration options which is great because I don’t care about Growl notifications, unread counts or the HUD component. As well as Twitter it handles a few other social networks side by side: Flickr and Facebook most notably.

It’s in beta so can be a little buggy, the text isn’t easy to read and I don’t like the way everything requires a read/unread status. I read everything from Twitter but definitely not from Facebook where I couldn’t care less if I’ve seen the update or not.
