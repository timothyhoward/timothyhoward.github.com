This map may look biased towards being close to my work but it really only covers about a 10km radius around the city, we don’t want to be in the CBD and my work just happens to be in a nice area.

We’re looking to rent a two bedroom apartment for around $400/week but our number one priority is close location to work. The green areas are all close. Our list of travel options in my order of preference:

  1. Cycling
  2. Walking
  3. Ferry (The City Cat, which Monocle rates highly)
  4. Train with no transfers

If I can’t commute to work within 15 or 20 minutes each way using those modes of transport the place isn’t suitable. That’s why we’ve chosen the areas we have. There’s a huge abundance of dedicated bike paths, train lines and ferry terminals lining the river.
