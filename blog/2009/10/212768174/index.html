My workplace has a liberal dress policy, you can just as easily get away with a nice shirt, pant and tie as you can shorts and a hoodie. What I’ve worn every day since starting there lands right between the two. A pair of slacks with a button down collared shirt which is sometimes tucked in, sometimes not depending on the combo and a hard-soled shoe.

I don’t mind this outfit, it’s basically comfortable and practical. But I don’t love it, outside of work I’m reluctant to wear long pants at all and I hate jeans. I ride to work so the hard shoe isn’t ideal, I need to roll up my pant cuffs and as we get towards the end of the year it’s only getting hotter.

Theoretically I prefer my current dress, if not something nicer, I like the idea of looking sharp. Practically, I’d be much more comfortable in shorts during the day and my commute would be more enjoyable but I’d look less professional. I’m leaning towards shorts.
