After seeing the irresistible Watchmen trailer (720p .mov) I saw the movie soon after it was released in cinemas. It didn’t live up to my extremely high expectations but that’s not to say I didn’t enjoy it.

Still, every so often I go back to the trailer and want to see the movie again. A friend from work lent me the novel and I loved it, much more than I remember enjoying the movie.

Today I watched the movie again. I had a greater appreciation and better understanding having seen it once before and read the novel but it still didn’t live up to its potential. Watchmen is an intricate and complex story, the movie leaves a significant amount of that out, which is definitely fine but it then adds fat elsewhere.

One thing the movie did get right is the opening credits. In five minutes it completely covers the background story in a series of gorgeous slow motion scenes. It’s beautifully succinct in a way the rest of the movie is not.
