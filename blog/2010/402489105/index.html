What I want from Channel 9’s Winter Olympics coverage:

  • Athletes performing in live Winter Olympic events.

What I get from Channel 9’s Winter Olympics coverage:

  • Interviews with Australian athletes by patronising hosts who belittle their profession by asking questions about the rules, how scary it must be and how cold snow is.
  • Analysis of events by panelists with little knowledge or even interest in Winter sports mostly spent marvelling at the scenery.
  • Special reports on the shopping and eating to be had in downtown Vancouver.
  • Long winded interviews with the people behind the opening ceremonies.
  • Special reports on the nightlife in Whistler.
